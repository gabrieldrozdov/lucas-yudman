LUCAS YUDMAN

Lucas Yudman is a New York based actor, improviser, and proud SAG-AFTRA Union member who takes pride in the mastery of his instrument. Raised by Just Voices Casting founder Maureen Kelly and Voice Actor/ Mock Rock Comedian Gary Yudman, Lucas was forged by an ever changing industry. From youth performances, to his education at SUNY Purchase, Magnet Theatre, UCB and beyond, Lucas has spent his life training his voice and body to become what many have described as “The next Tom Hanks.”

Lucas has spent years discovering how to tell stories in pictures under the tutelage of industry renowned acting coach Bob Krakower. Now a consistent member of his Master Class program, he has taken what he has learned into the scenes you see below.

Voice Over

Having booked several commercials in his youth and getting him started in SAG-AFTRA at a young age, Lucas retains his deep passion for the art of voice acting.

Improv/​Comedy

Studying Improv and Character work at Magnet Theater and UCB, Lucas remains in the New York Improv scene as one of the founding members of Loose Corn. Created in 2022, Loose Corn is a 13 person supergroup that has gone on to perform at The PIT, Young Ethel’s, and many other comedy venues.
